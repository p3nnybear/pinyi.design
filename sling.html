Sling TV is an American over-the-top internet television service owned by Dish Network​. In this specific project, I developed UI and voice/remote control integrations for Sling TV on the Google Nest Hub platform. We have the Sling TV: Live TV product intergrated on Google Nest Hub. Also, I designed Out-Of-Box Experience (OOBE) flows and conducted user tests to increase user retention and decrease user drop off rates

The Refinement

Testing & Iteration

Usability tests and metrics revealed that some of the process and design decisions I made were not as intuitive as I thought. Referring back to the personas, I knew that users heavily value a user-friendly product and would like to minimize the time spent on understanding it. As a result, I needed to iterate, test, and achieve measurable outcomes to overcome these product shortcomings.
